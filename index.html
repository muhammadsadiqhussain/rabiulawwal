About (PBUH)

Prophet Muhammad's (pbuh) personality was characterized by exceptional virtues including compassion, mercy, honesty, justice, patience, humility, forgiveness, and generosity. He was a model of excellent manners, always treating people, including his enemies and the vulnerable, with kindness and respect. He was known for his sincerity, conviction, and constant effort to bring joy to people, embodying an optimistic and virtuous life that served as a challenge to any doubter.

Hadith (PBUH)

01 "The best among you are those who have the best manners and character." - Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

02 "None of you truly believes until he loves for his brother what he loves for himself." — Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

03 "He who does not thank people, does not thank Allah." - Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

04 "Make things easy, not difficult. Spread peace, not hatred." - Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

05 "A smile is charity." - Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

What to Do on 12 Rabi-ul-Awwal:

  1. Recite Durood (Salawat) in abundance
  2. Read or listen to the Seerah (life) of the Prophet (PBUH)
  3. Recite the Qur'an
  4. Perform extra voluntary prayers and give charity
  5. Share a story or Hadith with family
